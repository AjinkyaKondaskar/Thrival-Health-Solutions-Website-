How Thrival Health fits into your clinic

  1. Clinic or Prescriber enters the order Add patient info, medications, and notes in the portal. The system generates a clean prescription PDF automatically.
  2. Prescriber signs & pays Only prescribers can sign and pay from their own account, so clinical and financial responsibility stay crystal clear.
  3. Partner pharmacy fills & ships Orders flow through our secure API to a partnering compounding pharmacy, which compounds and ships directly to the patient’s address—typically within 48 hours.
  4. Track shipping in real time Clinics and prescribers can follow each patient’s order from the moment it enters the pharmacy system to delivery at the patient’s doorstep using the built-in Shipping Info.

Built for Clinics and Prescribers

Keep clinical decisions with the prescriber while giving your staff a clean, repeatable workflow for every order.

  • Create and manage patient profiles securely.
  • Add medications and notes in a consistent format.
  • Preview the prescription PDF before it’s signed.
  • Follow order status from “created” to “shipped”.

Why clinics choose Thrival Health

Address guarantee

If a package is delivered to the wrong address, we arrange a replacement shipment at no additional cost to the clinic or patient.

Simplified billing

Thrival Health collects payment from prescribing doctors. You decide how to charge patients, and we handle paying the pharmacy on a predictable schedule.

Less phone and fax

Reduce back-and-forth with the pharmacy. Orders, notes, and clarifications live in one secure, searchable system.
